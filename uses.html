Here's the technology I use on a day to day basis! Inspired heavily by uses.tech and other people with pages similar to this.

Hardware

  • I compute with a conglomeration of computer parts and a MacBook Pro (13 inch, 2015);
  • I type on a custom keyboard (KBD67 Lite, Gateron Yellows) and mouse with a Razer Viper Ultimate;
  • I browse Reddit on an iPhone XS;
  • I make notes on an iPad 9th Generation with Apple Pencil;
  • I vaguely keep time with an Apple Watch Series 6;
  • I criticise music tastes with AKG K712 PROs, Q Acoustics 2020i speakers with a Marantz combo amplifier and AirPods 2;
  • I host with a Hetzner VPS and various Raspberry Pi's.

Software

  • I operate with Fedora 35 and GNOME (I use Linux btw);
  • I browse YouTube with Chromium and Firefox;
  • I code with Visual Studio Code;
  • I make music with Logic Pro;
  • I make graphics with the Affinity suite;
  • I invoice people with InvoiceNinja.

Services

  • I try to be productive with Google Workspace (email, office suite);
  • I serve websites with my own infrastructure, Vercel and GitHub Pages;
  • I listen to music with YouTube Music and my own CDs;
  • I manage domains with Cloudflare and Namecheap;
  • I manage versions with Git and GitHub.
