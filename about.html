Hi there! I'm Hayden, a fully self-taught full-stack developer, computer science student and learning graphics designer from the East Midlands, UK. This is my humble corner of the internet!

My interest in the world of computer science stems from a very early age, when I was known to tinker with old technology and take things apart to figure out their function. From then, my interest in programming and making computers do what I wanted grew into what it is today. On a more general scale, I'm also a fan of technology and its development in itself; it's not uncommon to see me getting excited about the next incremental update to some technology I find interesting.

My other interests include things like music, current affairs (along with the obligatory politics) and media like photography, video production and graphics design.

If you're interested, you can take a look at some of my recent projects and the technology I use day to day.
